Hello! I'm Mark Francis Ferrancullo, but I prefer the name Mana, as it holds sentimental value for me. You can call me Mark, Francis, or Franz, whichever you prefer; just don’t call me 'Mana' in real life. I strive to reach my holy grail of peace and serenity, currently working towards that by acquiring knowledge and experiences.

At 19, I’m pursuing a degree in IT at QCU, hoping to become a decent student. I love playing games, especially Tetris and rhythm games (like VSRGs). While I find academics stressful and often view them as obligations, I remain indifferent to them. I can be misanthropic and struggle with socializing, often feeling boring and awkward. However, I enjoy listening to music, watching movies, taking photographs, drawings, reading interesting things, and I have a fascination with insects like spiders and moths.
